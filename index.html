Kathryn E. Livingston

I’ve been writing about motherhood and parenting issues for more than 25 years. You can order my new memoir, Yin, Yang, Yogini: A Woman’s Quest for Balance, Strength and Inner Peace at Amazon.com or your favorite online bookseller. Yin, Yang, Yogini is a memoir about transformation through yoga, with yoga as the backdrop for change, a story of how one can evolve in mid-life and in mid-stride, of how one can learn to let go of the past, let go of fear, and live with trust in the present moment.

I hope you’ll join me on my writing and yoga journey. Please visit my personal blog (below) or scroll through this site: Pull out your mat, and let’s get moving! The river of life flows this way...
